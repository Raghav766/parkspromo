About Parks Promoters Charity

We are dedicated to promoting parks and natural spaces, in partnership with the Defenders of Wildlife Charity. Our goal is to preserve wildlife and protect ecosystems.

Our Mission

Our mission is to support the conservation of wildlife through the promotion of parks and protected areas. Together, we work with the Defenders of Wildlife Charity to advocate for endangered species and sustainable environments.
